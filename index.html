PRODUCTS

Faucets

Faucets are commonly found in households, public restrooms, and various other settings. Faucets are used for a wide range of applications, such as providing water for washing hands, bathing, cooking, cleaning etc.

Mirror

Bathroom mirrors are an essential component of any bathroom. They serve both functional and aesthetic purposes, allowing you to see yourself clearly while adding style and depth to the space. They enhance the look of bathrooms.

Showers

Showers provides a more efficient and convenient way to bathe compared to traditional methods such as using a bathtub or basin. Showers are commonly found in bathrooms and are available in various designs and configurations.

Wall Mixer

A wall mixer, also known as a wall-mounted mixer or wall-mounted faucet, is a type of plumbing fixture used in bathrooms and kitchens to control the flow and temperature of water. It is a very popular choice for its sleek and modern look.

Wash Basin

Washbasin is used for handwashing, face washing, and other personal grooming activities. It typically consists of a bowl, faucet, and drain. Washbasin come in various styles, sizes, and materials to match different bathroom aesthetics.

Geysers

In the context of home appliances, a geyser is a device used to heat water for various purposes, such as bathing, washing dishes, or other household needs. The geyser is an essential appliance s it provides continuous supply of hot water.
